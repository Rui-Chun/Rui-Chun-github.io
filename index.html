About Me

I am PhD student at Yale University, advised by Prof. Wenjun Hu. My research interests include Wireless Networking, Internet of Things (IoT), and RF Systems. I am currently working on smart (meta)surface systems, where I design and build end-to-end systems to support/enhance wireless communication and sensing.

I am actively seeking a researcher position or a postdoc. Please contact me if you know an opening. My CV can be found here.
