Biography

I am PhD student at Yale University, advised by Prof. Wenjun Hu. My research interests include Wireless Networking, Internet of Things (IoT), and Networkingg Systems. I am currently working on smart (meta)surface systems, where I design and build surface systems to support/enhance wireless networks.
